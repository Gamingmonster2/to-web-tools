# 🚀 TO Web Tools

Professional browser-based tools that run entirely on your device.

---

## 🧠 About

TO Web Tools is a collection of fast, private, and powerful tools that work directly in your browser without uploading any data.

All processing happens locally (Client-Side), ensuring maximum privacy and speed.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🔒 100% Client-Side (no uploads)
* ⚡ Fast and lightweight
* 🧰 Multiple tools in one place
* 🎯 Simple and clean interface
* 🌐 Works in any modern browser

---

## 🧰 Available Tools

### 🎥 Video Recorder

Record high-quality video from your webcam with audio directly in your browser.

---

### 🖼️ Image Editor

Resize and crop images quickly without losing quality.

---

### 🎨 Blob Generator

Generate random organic shapes in SVG format for design projects.

---

### 🎮 Gold Miner Game

A simple fun game to test your reaction speed.

---

## 🎯 Why Use This?

In today's digital world, privacy matters.

TO Web Tools ensures that:

* Your files never leave your device
* No data is stored on servers
* Everything runs locally in your browser
